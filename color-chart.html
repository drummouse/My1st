Material Note
All finishes are factory-applied to steel coil — field painting voids warranty. RAL codes are the definitive colour reference. On-screen swatches are calibrated against physical samples but are not binding. Order a sample pack before specifying.
Warranty & Rating
25-year manufacturer's paint warranty on Thyssen-Krupp substrate finishes. IronWrap labour warranty issued at contract signing. Class 4 impact rating may qualify your client for homeowner insurance premium reduction — confirm with insurer.
Lead Times
Stocked colours ship from Edmonton (10743 180 St NW). Non-stock RAL specials: 3–5 week mill run minimum. Printech Woodgrain composites: 2–3 weeks from DaiDong. All codes on this chart (RAL, Wood, RYM-01) are confirmed manufacturer references.
