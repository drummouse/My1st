Material Note

All steel finishes are factory-applied. Field painting voids warranty. RAL codes are the definitive color reference — on-screen renderings are calibrated but not binding. Request physical samples before specifying.

Warranty

25-year manufacturer's paint warranty on Thyssen-Krupp substrate finishes. IronWrap installation warranty provided separately at contract signing. Class 4 impact rating may qualify for homeowner insurance premium reduction.

Lead Time

Standard colors: stock held at 10743 180 St NW, Edmonton. Non-stocked RAL specials: 3–5 week mill run minimum. Wood-grain composite panels: 2–3 weeks from DaiDong. Contact shop directly for current inventory.
